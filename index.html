Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know: Mola’s Most Common Queries
What is Mola ?
Mola is a peer-to-peer currency exchange platform tailored for African immigrants and locals. It allows users to exchange their local currencies for international currencies, such as Pounds, Dollars, and Euros, at competitive rates with minimal or no transaction fees. Mola provides a secure, transparent, and efficient way to manage currency exchanges by connecting buyers and sellers directly.
How do I Sell on Mola ?
To sell currency on Mola, simply create an account, complete all the verification requirement, Top up your wallet, navigate to the ‘Ads’ section, and post an ad with your offered exchange rate and currency amount and wait for offers from different buyers. Mola’s escrow system securely holds the posted amount until the transaction is completed, ensuring a safe and seamless exchange.
Which Currency can I exchange on Mola ?
Our platform is regularly updated to expand the range of supported currencies. Currently, we support one international currency, the Pound, which can be exchanged against one African currency, the Naira.
How can I add funds to my Mola wallet?
Topping up your Mola wallet is simple. You can add funds through a variety of payment methods, including bank transfers, mobile money, and other supported financial services. Just log in to your account, go to the 'Wallet' section, and follow the prompts to top up your balance.
How do I buy currency on Mola?
To purchase currency on Mola, start by exploring the listings in the 'Buy' section. Compare the available rates and choose the offer that best fits your needs. Click the 'Buy' button, enter the amount you wish to purchase, and follow the instructions to transfer the funds to the account provided on the order page. After completing the transfer, confirm your payment, and the seller will verify the transaction, crediting your account promptly.
